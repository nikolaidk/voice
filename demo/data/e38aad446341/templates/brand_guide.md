# Brand style guide — Nordlys Consulting
- Look: clean, light, Scandinavian corporate. White background, plenty of whitespace.
- Primary text: near-black ink #1A2332. Secondary text: slate gray #64748B.
- Accent color: fjord teal #0E7C7B — used sparingly for emphasis and markers.
- Headings: elegant serif (Georgia-like). Body: neutral sans-serif.
- Slide text: sentence case only, never ALL CAPS. Max 3 bullets per slide, short and declarative.
